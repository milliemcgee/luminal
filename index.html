The Practice

Practiced.
Present.
Unhurried.

Luminal was established to offer something that does not easily fit into existing categories — a professional, considered, and genuinely beautiful ceremony for the energetic life of a home.

The Philosophy

We are not a cleaning service.
We are not a religious ritual.

What we offer is closer to the work of a skilled restorer — someone who understands that what is invisible in a space is as real as what you can touch, and that it can be transformed with the right skill, attention, and intention.

The ceremonies we carry out draw on a range of traditions: the ancient practice of smoke cleansing, the use of sound to shift the atmosphere of a space, the placement of protective and restorative materials, and the careful setting of intention through ceremony and ritual.

None of these approaches require a particular set of beliefs. They require only an openness to the possibility that the atmosphere of a space can change — and a desire for it to do so.

Frequently Asked Questions

On the practice,
the process,
and what to expect.

Space clearing is the practice of shifting the energetic atmosphere of a place — clearing residual energy left by previous occupants, difficult events, or the natural accumulation of time, and restoring a quality of calm and clarity to the space. It draws on traditions from many cultures across many centuries, all of which share the understanding that the atmosphere of a space is shaped by what has happened within it — and that it can be changed.
No. Many of our clients are not particularly spiritual and approach the service from a place of curiosity, openness, or simply a practical desire for their home to feel different. The ceremony involves intention, attention, and beautiful sensory materials — all of which have a measurable effect on how a space feels, regardless of one’s metaphysical framework.
Each ceremony is tailored to the space and the situation. Most involve a combination of smoke cleansing, sound clearing with crystal singing bowls and bells, the use of protective and restorative botanical and crystal materials, and an intention-setting ceremony specific to your home and circumstances. A pre-appointment consultation shapes the approach; the ceremony itself unfolds with the space.
This depends on the service. A single-room clearing takes between one and two hours. A full property clearing for a large home may take four to five hours. We will give you a clear estimate during your consultation before any booking is confirmed.
We ask that the property is as tidy as possible and that the atmosphere on the day is quiet — ideally, only you and any immediate household members present. Please do not deep-clean immediately before the appointment. We will send you a full pre-ceremony preparation guide when your booking is confirmed.
Some ceremonies involve smoke cleansing with ethically sourced dried botanicals. The scent is gentle and dissipates within a few hours. All windows will be opened during the ceremony. If you have a medical sensitivity to smoke, please mention this at enquiry and we will adapt our approach accordingly.
We make no medical claims and do not claim to diagnose, treat, or cure any condition. We do not claim to remove supernatural entities or guarantee specific outcomes. What we offer is a skilled, considered, and intentional ceremony that, in our experience and in the experience of our clients, creates a noticeable and meaningful shift in the atmosphere of a space.
Every client receives a written aftercare guide within 48 hours. This includes recommendations specific to your property, suggested crystals or botanicals to maintain the clarity of the space, and guidance for the season ahead. A follow-up clarity call is available within four weeks as an optional add-on.
Our primary service area is the South West of England and surrounding counties — Bath, Bristol, Somerset, Wiltshire, Dorset, Gloucestershire, and the Cotswolds. We travel further by arrangement. Remote ceremonies are available to clients anywhere in the UK and internationally.
Our fees begin at £85 for object clearing and range to £1,400 for large commercial properties. Full pricing is available on our Services page or shared personally during an enquiry call. A 50% deposit is required to secure all bookings, with the balance due 48 hours before the ceremony.
